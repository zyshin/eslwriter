How To Use

ESLWriter has collected nearly all the paper in the field of computer science (and more other domains in the near future) for you to refer to. It features three fascinating functions: Field Setting, Collocation Finding, Semantic Searching.

Field Setting

Sign up as a registered user and click your profile on the top to the account setting page. Choose your interested field as the corpus.

Collocation Finding

Enter one or two keywords and the search bar will automatically show collocation tips to choose. It supports (修饰)/(动宾)/(介词)/(主谓) of up to two collocated words to search.

searching for a verb to collocate with robustness

Searching for a preposition for suggestion

Semantic Searching

Type a word you would like to find synonyms and add a question mark ? in the end. Then you would get the synonyms of this word in the related field.

Mixed Search

Finally, it has also mixed search function to combine semantic searching and collocation finding functions. After selecting a collocation tip you can add a question mark after one or two key words to have better results.
